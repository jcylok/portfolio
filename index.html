About Me

I'm a full stack developer with a Master degree in Applied Analytics from Columbia University. Having some years of QA experience in the tech industry from previous careers shaped me into an effective team member. Now, as a developer I always strive for the best outcomes and learn new technologies to create innovative and scalable applications.

Sometimes, it’s hard not to compare myself with my former co-workers, and friends in this industry, who are 1000 times more successful than me. I truly understand that everyone is unique, and we need not compare ourselves with others. Rather than letting the negative emotion demoralize me, I always convert it to my motivation. “Chance favors the prepared mind.” While the successful stories of my peers are my motivator, being prepared is my all-time attitude. A prepared mind does not necessarily lead to success, but a successful person is always prepared. I will keep at doing my best!

When I'm not coding, I like playing piano, singing karaoke, and sharing music to my beloved ones.
