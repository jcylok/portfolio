About Me

I'm a full stack developer with a Master degree in Applied Analytics from Columbia University. Having some years of QA experience from my previous career in a giant search engine company shaped me into an effective team member. Now, as a developer I always strive for the best outcomes and learn new technologies to create innovative and scalable applications.

When I'm not coding, I like playing piano, singing karaoke, and sharing music to my beloved ones.

Sometimes, it’s hard not to compare myself with my former co-workers, and friends in this industry, who are 1000 times more successful than me. I truly understand that everyone is unique, and we need not compare ourselves with others. Rather than letting the negative emotion demoralize me, I always convert it to my motivation. “Chance favors the prepared mind.” While the successful stories of my peers are my motivator, being prepared is my all-time attitude. A prepared mind does not necessarily lead to success, but a successful person is always prepared. I will keep at doing my best!

Nook & Novel

Previous Next
Group Project (2)
Technologies Used: Javascript, Express, MongoDB

Nook & Novel was created to allow people to share knowledge and grow together through books. We decided to pair our book tracking idea with map functionality that allows you to search an area for coffee stores. You can search for books in our library and add them to a list of “books you want to read,” “books you’ve read,” and “books you’d recommend to others.” These lists are updated and presented on your profile for others to see. Delievered within 7 days.

OKStupid

Group Project (3)
Technologies Used: Python, Django, PostgreSQL

OKStupid is a dating app that allows users to explore people around their area. Users are asked to provide information such as gender, age, and customized prompts before getting access to the search feature. The search result will return the people that meet user's criteria. Two persons are connected when they both 'like' each other. This app was completed within 7 days.

Space Invader

Solo Project
Technologies Used: javascript, HTML, CSS, jQuery

Space Invader is a movement game that challenges the user to improve their hand-eye coordination. User can choose to play in single player mode or double players mode. User earns points by destroying the enemies and collecting coins. A astronaut willy fly from the the bottom when only one life is left. User can collect it to heal.
